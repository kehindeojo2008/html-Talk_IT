TALK IT OUT AI Privacy Policy

Effective: November 20, 2025

At TALK IT OUT, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy, especially concerning sensitive personal information related to mental health. Our Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our websites and applications (the TALK IT OUT AI website), and other TALK IT OUT AI services (our "Service"). It also describes your privacy rights.

1. About TALK IT OUT AI

Talk It Out is a service designed to provide AI-powered conversational support, mental wellness resources, a premium sound lounge, and an anonymous community forum to users seeking mental wellness support.

2. Personal Information We Collect (Notice at Collection)

Notice: We ask that you DO NOT include identifiable medical records, specific diagnoses, or the full names of third parties in your inputs.

Data Category How We May Collect It How We May Use It How We May Disclose It
Account & Profile Data Directly from you (e.g., email, name). To provide, analyze, and maintain the Service; manage subscriptions. To contracted service providers; for legal purposes.
Payment Data Through third-party processors (Apple, Google, Stripe). To process subscription payments. To third-party payment processors.
User Content & Chat Data Directly from text or voice inputs/prompts. To provide personalized AI conversation; improve/train AI models. To contracted service providers. (Note: Anonymized for training).
Community Data Directly from posts/comments in Anonymous Community. Facilitate community experience; monitor for safety. To other community users; safety monitoring providers.
Technical & Usage Data Automatically when you use the Service (IP, device type). Monitor/enforce free-tier limits; develop and improve Service. To analytics and hosting providers.

Regarding Sensitive Personal Information: While our service relates to mental health, we do not actively seek to collect "sensitive personal information" unless you voluntarily choose to include them in your User Content.

3. How We May Use Personal Information

  • To Provide the Service: Delivering personalized chat and Sound Lounge access.
  • To Manage Subscriptions: Processing payments and enforcing usage limits.
  • To Improve Our AI Models: Refinement via anonymized and aggregated data.
  • Safety and Security: Detecting fraud and monitoring for harmful content.

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information: We do not sell personal information or share it for targeted advertising.

4. Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service or fulfill legal obligations.

  • Chat Data: Retained for 30 to 90 days, then anonymized or deleted.
  • Account Data: Retained while account is active; deleted within 30 days of request.
